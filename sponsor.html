Challenges in Healthcare Access

Understanding the barriers faced by epilepsy patients in accessing needed healthcare services and medications is crucial. Details on high costs, insurance complexities, and limited access to specialized care are common challenges that hinder effective management of epilepsy.

Policy Advocacy for Better Health Coverage

Highlighting the critical role of advocacy in shaping policies that ensure comprehensive coverage for epilepsy care. Efforts from various organizations have led to improvements in health policy, aiming to cover all aspects of epilepsy management, including medication, therapy, and emergency care.
